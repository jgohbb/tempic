Welcome to Tempic, the coolest (or maybe hottest) temperature based, city arranging game on the internet! Ok, it might be the only one. Who cares? It's fun. Here's how you play:
  • 5 city cards will be presented randomly from a list of 20 possible options.
  • Your challange is to arrange the cities in order from coolest to warmest.
  • You will have a limited number of tries to guess the order correctly.
  • And, just for fun, we added a button to each card which provides additional city information.
  • Enter your name and click 'Let's Play' to begin!
